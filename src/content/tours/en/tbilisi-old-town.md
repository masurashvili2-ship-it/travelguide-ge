We start at **Liberty Square** and wind through the **Old Town** toward the **Abanotubani** sulfur bath district. You will see a mix of Persian, European, and local architecture, street art, and viewpoints over the Mtkvari.

**Highlights**

- Narikala cable car viewpoint (optional add-on)
- Legvtakhevi canyon and waterfall
- Tips for supper spots and natural wine bars

Wear comfortable shoes; cobblestones can be slippery when wet.
