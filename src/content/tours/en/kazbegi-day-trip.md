A full-day route along the **Georgian Military Highway** with stops at **Zhinvali Reservoir**, **Ananuri** complex, and the **Jvari Pass** viewpoint before reaching **Stepantsminda**.

The final climb to **Gergeti Trinity Church** (4WD or hike) offers iconic views of **Mount Kazbek** when skies are clear.

**Note:** Mountain weather changes quickly—bring layers and check road conditions in winter.
