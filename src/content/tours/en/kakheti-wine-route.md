Kakheti is Georgia’s eastern wine region. This route balances **small family wineries** with a historic monastery stop and a relaxed **supra-style** lunch (vegetarian options available on request).

You will learn the basics of **qvevri** fermentation and taste both amber and red styles typical of the area.
